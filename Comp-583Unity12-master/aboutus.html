About Us

Our Story

Unity Services was founded in 2023 by a group of friends who saw a need for reliable on-demand services in their community. As they looked around, they noticed that many people were struggling to find trustworthy professionals for home repairs, cleaning, and other services.

With a shared passion for providing excellent customer service and a commitment to using only certified professionals, they decided to create Unity Services to connect people with the services they need, when they need them.

Our Mission

At Unity Services, our mission is to make life easier for our customers by providing high-quality on-demand services at a fair price. We believe that everyone deserves access to reliable professionals who can help them with tasks big and small.

We are dedicated to building long-lasting relationships with our customers by providing exceptional service and support every step of the way. Whether you need a plumber, electrician, or house cleaner, we've got you covered.

Our Team

Our team of professionals is made up of experienced and certified experts who are passionate about what they do. We carefully screen and vet every professional we work with to ensure that they meet our high standards for quality and reliability.

From our customer service representatives to our service providers, everyone at Unity Services is committed to providing the best possible experience for our customers.
